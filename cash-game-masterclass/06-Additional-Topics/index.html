Key Takeaways

  • Shorthanded: Ranges widen only slightly (~4-5%). Not a radically different game.
  • Heads-up: Dramatically different. Raise 81% on button, play 75% from BB. Mixed strategies matter.
  • UTG Straddle: Never straddle yourself. Trap wild straddlers with premium hands.
  • Mississippi Straddle: Blinds get crushed. Fold a lot from the blinds.
  • Short-stacked: Use tournament charts as a base, play tighter due to rake. Blockers > suited connectors.
  • Three-blind: Small and middle blind must 3-bet or fold. Effective stack is halved.
