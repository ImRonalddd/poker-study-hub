Recommended Study Path

Phase 1 — Core Preflop and Sizing

  1. Mastering Preflop Play — Lock in GTO ranges for both 8-max live and 6-max online. Every postflop decision begins with a sound preflop range.
  2. Advanced Bet Sizing — Learn how nut advantage, range connectivity, and board dynamics determine your bet size on every street.

Phase 2 — Multiway and Deep-Stacked Play

  1. Advanced Multiway Strategies — Adjust your heads-up instincts for multiway pots. Learn why you check more, bet smaller, and tighten your value range.
  2. Postflop Deep-Stacked Adjustments — Understand how stack depth shifts nut advantage, position value, and stack-off thresholds dramatically.

Phase 3 — GTO Meets Exploitation

  1. GTO Concepts for Live Cash Games — Bridge solver theory into actionable live cash decisions with Justin Saliba's framework.
  2. High Stakes Live Cash Game Secrets — See how Chris Brewer simplifies GTO for the biggest games and when he deviates to exploit.
  3. Mastering Exploits in 6-Max Online — Brad Wilson's systematic approach to identifying and attacking opponent imbalances.

Phase 4 — Heads-Up Mastery

  1. Crushing Heads Up NL Hold'em — The ultimate test of your fundamentals. Build robust HUNL ranges and study real match analysis at 100bb and 200bb depths.

Ongoing Practice

After completing each module, work through its quiz to test retention. Revisit the quick reference card before sessions. The four-instructor format means you will see the same concepts from different angles — use that overlap to deepen understanding rather than skipping familiar topics.
