Chapter 1

Mastering Preflop Play in Cash Games

Instructor: Jonathan Little — 20 Lessons

What you'll learn: Complete GTO preflop strategies for 8-max live (100bb/200bb, no rake) and 6-max online (100bb, 5% rake). Covers every preflop decision tree from RFI through 6-bet all-in.
  • Raise First In ranges for all positions and stack depths
  • 3-bet, 4-bet, and 5-bet strategies (both as aggressor and defender)
  • Squeezing, cold 4-betting, and multiway dynamics
  • Blind vs blind play and high-action spots
  • Exploitative adjustments for live and online games
